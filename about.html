Behind The Innovation

Learn about our innovative approach to pangasius farming using automation technology

Our Story

Our journey began with a simple goal to make fish farming smarter, easier, and more sustainable. We noticed that many local farmers still rely on manual water monitoring, which takes time and often leads to inconsistent results.

To address this, we created the Smart Water Quality Management System for tank-based Cream Dory farming. Using Arduino and ESP32 with a Recirculating Aquaculture System (RAS), it tracks key parameters like pH, temperature, salinity, dissolved oxygen, ammonia, and water level in real time.

What began as a simple idea has become a working system that blends technology, teamwork, and sustainability, showing that innovation can truly transform local aquaculture and help strengthen the future of Pangasius production in the Philippines.

Background

To address the challenges of manual fish farming, our project introduces an automated water quality monitoring system for tank-based Cream Dory. It promotes smarter, more efficient, and sustainable aquaculture in the Philippines.

Objective

To develop a smart system that monitors water parameters such as pH, temperature, salinity, dissolved oxygen, ammonia, and water level in real time, improving productivity and supporting sustainable fish farming.

System Overview

The system uses Arduino and ESP32 microcontrollers integrated with a Recirculating Aquaculture System (RAS). It automates data collection and displays real-time results through a web-based dashboard and LCD display.

Significance

This project helps local fish farmers maintain ideal water conditions, reduce fish loss, and improve yield. It supports sustainable aquaculture practices and could strengthen Pangasius production and export potential in the Philippines.
